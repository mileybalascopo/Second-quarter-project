Pisay connect is an enlightening educational series within the Philippine Science High School (PSHS) community that aims to connect all campuses of the Philippine Science High School. The series features insightful tours and interviews that provides an in-depth look into each campus, guided by the perspectives of the esteemed Campus Directors. Additionally, there are also episodes wherein various professionals share their experiences and contributions within the STEM community.
